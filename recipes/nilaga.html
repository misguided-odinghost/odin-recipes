Nilaga Recipe

Nilaga is a traditional Filipino boiled meat soup dish that is typically made with beef or pork, along with various vegetables such as potatoes, cabbage, and green beans. It is a comforting and hearty meal, perfect for cold weather.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg beef shank or pork belly, cut into chunks
  • 1 medium onion, quartered
  • 4 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, sliced
  • 2 medium potatoes, peeled and quartered
  • 1 small cabbage, cut into wedges
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed
  • 8 cups water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, combine the beef or pork, onion, garlic, and ginger. Pour in the water and bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce heat and simmer for 1.5 to 2 hours, or until the meat is tender.
  3. Add the potatoes and continue to simmer for another 10 minutes.
  4. Add the cabbage and green beans, and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.
