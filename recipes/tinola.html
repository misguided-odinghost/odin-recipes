Tinolang Manok


Tinola is a traditional Filipino chicken soup dish that is typically made with chicken, green papaya, and chili leaves. It is flavored with ginger, garlic, and fish sauce, making it a comforting and nutritious meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • 1 medium green papaya, peeled and sliced
  • 1 bunch of chili leaves (or substitute with spinach)
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 medium onion, sliced
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 2 tbsp fish sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, sauté garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken pieces and cook until they are lightly browned.
  3. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through.
  4. Add the sliced green papaya and continue to simmer for another 10 minutes or until the papaya is tender.
  5. Season with fish sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the chili leaves and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until wilted.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
