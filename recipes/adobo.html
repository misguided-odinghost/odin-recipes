Adobong Baboy


Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made with meat, marinated in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and spices, then simmered until tender. It is known for its rich and savory flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 head garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp black peppercorns
  • 1 cup water
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and black peppercorns. Add the pork belly and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat cooking oil over medium heat. Sauté the onion until translucent.
  3. Add the marinated pork along with the marinade. Cook for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add water and bay leaves. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 45 minutes to 1 hour, or until the pork is tender and the sauce has thickened.
  5. Season with salt to taste. Serve hot with steamed rice.
